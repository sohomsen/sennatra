Dum Maro Dum by Asha Bhosle and R. D. Burman is one of those Bollywood records that’s instantly recognizable across generations. It carries a certain energy and nostalgia that never fades, and it has always held a special place in my heart.

<br />
One night at Bricolage in Brooklyn, I heard a track playing in the restaurant
that sampled the iconic Dum Maro Dum guitar riff. The moment I recognized it,
something clicked. I thought, why not flip this myself?

<br />
What started as a passing idea stayed with me. For months, I kept thinking about
how I would approach it, how I could respect the original while bringing my own
sound into it. Eventually, I stopped overthinking and just made the remix.
Sometimes inspiration hits instantly. Execution just takes patience.
