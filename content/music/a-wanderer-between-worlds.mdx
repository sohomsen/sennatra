This EP marks the beginning of my journey as sennatra. It brings together a collection of records I produced throughout 2025, capturing the sound and direction that started to define me as an artist.

<br />

A Wanderer Between Worlds is built around a simple concept shaped by the track titles. I imagined a character moving through unfamiliar spaces, both physical and emotional. The opening track, “Void Walker,” sets the tone. I pictured someone from beyond our world stepping into the unknown, walking through open space with no clear destination.

<br />

That wanderer eventually arrives at “Echo Valley,” a place filled with distant voices and shifting sounds. As he explores, one voice stands out and pulls him into a trance. That moment becomes “Raag Mirage,” a spell-like experience that blurs reality and illusion. When the haze fades, he wakes up in the middle of a loud market. Beside him is a candy shop. That scene became “Mitha Bazaar.”

<br />

Fun fact: I named the track “Mitha Bazaar” because the song reminded me of “Candy Shop” by 50 Cent. “Mitha” translates to “candy” in Hindi, and “bazaar” is another word for “market” or “shop.” It felt like a subtle nod while still tying back to the story and my cultural roots.
